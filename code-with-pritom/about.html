About Us

Get to know the story behind "<Code With Pritom/>" and its commitment to the tech community.

Meet Arup Bhowmik Pritom

Hello! I'm Arup Bhowmik Pritom, a passionate Computer Science & Engineering graduate from East West University (EWU). My journey in tech has been fueled by a deep curiosity for innovation and a desire to share knowledge with fellow students and enthusiasts.

"<Code With Pritom/>" was born from this passion – a vision to create a central hub where CSE students across Bangladesh can find valuable resources, stay updated on university news, discover learning opportunities, and connect with a vibrant community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower CSE students by providing accessible, high-quality information and fostering a collaborative learning environment. We aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world tech applications, helping students prepare for successful careers.

What We Offer

  • Curated CSE department news from universities nationwide.
  • A dedicated and prioritized content stream for East West University students.
  • Timely updates on free seminars, workshops, and tech meetups.
  • Practical programming tips, code snippets, and best practices.
  • Useful PC tricks, productivity hacks, and troubleshooting guides.
  • Insights into digital marketing, SEO, and SMM trends.

We are constantly evolving and welcome your suggestions to make "<Code With Pritom/>" even better. Join us on this exciting journey!
